
# Renewing Certs
![[Pasted image 20250524181014.png]]
## Earn CEU from employment (max:9)
-  3 CEU p/year
- Needs to be submitted yearly
#### How to submit ?
- **Timing**: The work experience must be completed **during your three-year renewal cycle**.  
    Submission timeframe:
    - End of year 1 of CE 3-year cycle 
    - End of year 2 of CE 3-year cycle
    - End of year 3 of CE 3-year cycle
- **Documentation**: To receive CEUs for your work experience, you will need to [submit](https://www.certmetrics.com/comptia/login.aspx) a letter from your employer on company letterhead containing the following information:
    1. Your name
    2. Current job title
    3. The Continuing Education (CE) year for which your work experience is being submitted
    4. Tasks you performed
    5. Signature of your supervisor

## Earn CEU from Webinar (max:15)
**Documentation:** Submit the following documentation to receive CEUs for the webinar:

- Your name
- Name of the webinar
- Date of the webinar
- Number of hours
- Copy of Email
- CPD Word Document outline - What is about / What learned/
