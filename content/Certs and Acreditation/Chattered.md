## Goal
To earn and become Chartered Cyber Security Professional (ChCSP)
# UK Cyber Sec Council
[Cyber Security Council PDF](https://www.ukcybersecuritycouncil.org.uk/media/un0j1eur/v-4-21-12-22-uk-csc-spcc.pdf)
### Requirements
- Plan, Record and submit CPDs in a professional manner (online template)
- [Become-professionally-registered](https://www.ukcybersecuritycouncil.org.uk/professional-standards-registration/become-professionally-registered/)
To become ChCSP one needs to earn certification from one of the licensed bodies  
#### [CREST](https://www.crest-approved.org/)
- £700 for Security Testing - Penetration Testing
#### [The Cyber Scheme – TCS](https://thecyberscheme.org/professional-registration/)
